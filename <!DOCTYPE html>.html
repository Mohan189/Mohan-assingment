Biography

Arijit Singh is an acclaimed Indian playback singer known for his mesmerizing voice and emotional renditions. Born on April 25, 1987, in Jiaganj, West Bengal, he gained fame with his debut song "Phir Mohabbat" in the film Murder 2. Arijit's soulful and versatile singing style has won him numerous awards and widespread recognition. He has delivered chart-topping hits in Bollywood, including songs like "Tum Hi Ho," "Gerua," and "Ae Dil Hai Mushkil." Arijit Singh's talent and passion for music have made him one of the most sought-after and beloved singers in the Indian music industry. Music

Arijit Singh has been associated with numerous popular and chart-topping songs that have captivated audiences with his soulful voice. Some of his most notable and beloved tracks include "Tum Hi Ho" from the film Aashiqui 2, "Gerua" from Dilwale, "Ae Dil Hai Mushkil" from the movie of the same name, and "Channa Mereya" from Ae Dil Hai Mushkil. Other popular songs by Arijit Singh include "Agar Tum Saath Ho," "Muskurane," "Raabta," "Tera Ban Jaunga," and "Kabira." His ability to infuse emotions into his singing and his range of vocal expressions have made him a favorite among music lovers across the globe.
